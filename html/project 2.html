Linus Torvald

The creator of linux

Biography

Linus Torvald is a Finnish-American software enginner who is the creator and principal developer of the linus kernel for a numberof operating systems.He also created the version control system Git.Torvald was born on december 28,1969 in Helenski,Finland.He studied computer science at the University of Helsinki, and he developed the linux kernel as a student aproject in 1991.Since then, the linux kernel has become one of the most widely used kernels in the world, powrring everything from smatphones to supercomputers.
